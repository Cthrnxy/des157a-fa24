Alice's wonderland

Alice finds herself lost in Wonderland, trying to uncover clues for her next adventure. As she wanders through the mysterious forest, she comes across the Cheshire Cat, lounging lazily on a branch.

“Well, hello there, dear Alice,” the Cheshire Cat says with a sly grin. “I happen to have a clue you need, but if you want it, you'll have to beat me in a game.”

“What kind of game?” Alice asks, intrigued.

“The Pig Game,” the Cat replies, its tail curling playfully. “If you win, I'll give you the clue. But if I win...” The Cheshire Cat's grin grows wider. “You'll have to solve an even trickier riddle.”

Curiosity piqued, Alice agrees to the challenge.
